# Product

## Register

product

## Users

AIDEFEND Discovery is used by reviewers evaluating public discovery candidates before anything is treated as approved AIDEFEND knowledge. They work from generated `gap_run_*.json` reports, compare candidates against existing `AID-*` techniques, inspect citations and provenance, and record review decisions that remain distinct from backend recommendations.

## Product Purpose

The product finds, normalizes, scores, and reviews candidate additions or mappings for the AIDEFEND knowledge base without mutating canonical framework data. Success means a reviewer can quickly understand why a candidate surfaced, decide whether to promote, merge, reject, hold for evidence, or monitor it, and export reviewed decisions with enough provenance for audit.

## Brand Personality

Restrained, trustworthy, evidence-led. The interface should feel like a serious review workbench for security practitioners: dense, calm, legible, and careful about separating hypotheses from approved truth.

## Anti-references

Avoid marketing landing pages, decorative SaaS dashboards, raw JSON-first presentation, overclaimed AI certainty, dark-mode spectacle, oversized hero metrics, and any surface that blurs discovery candidates into canonical `AID-*` techniques.

## Design Principles

1. Human judgment first: scoring and recommendations guide review, but reviewer decisions stay explicit and separate.
2. Provenance on demand: citations, hashes, scores, and raw details are always available, but secondary to the human-readable brief.
3. Candidate namespace discipline: discovery output is candidate-only until promoted upstream.
4. Dense without panic: laptop-first layouts should support scanning, comparison, and repeated decisions without visual noise.
5. Audit-ready by default: exports and saved decisions carry enough context to reconstruct why a decision was made.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA for contrast, focus states, keyboard navigation, labels, and form controls. Do not rely on color alone for action or severity meaning. Keep motion minimal and respect reduced-motion preferences.
